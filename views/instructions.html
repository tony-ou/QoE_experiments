Instructions for the test

In this test, you will watch a series of short videos (~20 seconds each) and rate their quality. Please pay close attention to the following guidelines:

  • All the videos are created from a reference video with manually added impairments (e.g. stalls, resolution change, etc. However, you shouldn't consider stalls caused by your network conditions.). So you should rate each video based on how it compares with the reference video.
  • Please be sure to watch each video in its full length. We can tell if you skip a video. And please don't refresh the video page when watching. If you have to refresh because network fails, be sure to report this on the last page.
  • You will watch the reference video first and you need to rate it as Excellent quality.
  • The survey will end with a question like "what is the quality difference between the test videos". You need to give a reasonable answer such as by mentioning what impairments you saw and describing why you rated some test videos higher than others in general language. (meaning you don't have to recollect details of the videos)

Please take the simple survey before the test.
