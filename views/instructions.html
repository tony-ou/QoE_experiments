Instructions for the test

The goal of the experiment is to study how video impairments damage viewers' experience. We created a series of test videos (~20 seconds each) with same content but different quality impairments. Your job is to compare the test and reference video, and judge how much downgraded the test video is from the reference video. Please pay close attention to the following guidelines:

  • Test videos are created from a same reference video. You will watch the reference video first and you must rate it as Imperceptible Impairment (Perfect quality).
  • It might be the case the reference video itself has some quality issues. But these are not the concerns of this experiment. Hence, your rating should be solely based the difference btw the reference and test videos.
  • On the grading page, you will also be asked to select what impairments you saw in the test video. (This is an attention test).
  • Please be sure to watch each video in its full length. The button to the next page appears after you finish the video. And please don't refresh video page. If you have to because video fails to load, please report on the last page.

Please take the simple survey before the test.
