Instructions for the test

The goal of the experiment is to gauge how vudei impairments damage viewers' experience. You will watch a series of test videos (~20 seconds each) created from a reference video with additional quality impairments. You should focus on the difference btw test and reference videos, and decide how impairments damage the video. Please pay close attention to the following guidelines:

  • An impairment can be any temporal or spatial distortion. Some examples are bluring, stalling, tiling, etc.
  • You will watch the reference video first and you must rate it as Imperceptible Impairment. Again, this doesn't mean the reference video is in perfect quality, but just that there's no additional impairments.
  • On the grading page, you will also be asked to select what impairments you saw in the test video. (This is an attention test.
  • Please be sure to watch each video in its full length. The button to the next page appears after you finish the video. And please don't refresh video page. If you have to because video fails to load, please report on the last page.

Please take the simple survey before the test.
