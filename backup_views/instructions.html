Instructions for the test

In this test, you will watch a series of short videos (~20 seconds each) with same content but different levels of impairment.

The first video is the reference video without any impairment, and other videos have mannually added impairments such as video stalls, resolution change, etc. (These are just examples and may or may not be present in the videos).

After watching each video, please follow the instruction on the next page to answer the question about video quality. Follow your immediate impression and don't spend too long thinking.

Please watch each video in its ENTIRETY and answer each question completely and honestly. Please don't REFRESH the video page. If you have to refresh due to network problem, please report in the last page. You may not be paid if these conditions are not met.

Please take the simple survey before the test.

We use the results to look for trends in responses.
